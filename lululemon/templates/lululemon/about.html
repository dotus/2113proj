About

lululemon store Inventory Management System is a real-time data capturing system to assist in-store operations with replenishment and inventory control.

For store managers/assistant store managers, please log in to your administration site for:

- Item management: log new items with item information, manage existing items.
- Staff management: register new staffs, assign/unassign items to staff.

For educators, please log in to this system for:

- Item management: log new items with item information.
- View logged activities: view your assigned items.
- Personal information management: see your profile, change password.
